Who We Are

Job Seeker

As any experienced jobseeker can tell, there are plenty of recruitment consultancies out there but, with each varying significantly in terms of experience, expertise, and style, how do you find the one that’s right for you? To do that, luckily Power Hunt is here for you. ​ Job roles are increasingly specialized, and it’s frustrating for a candidate to talk to someone who claims to have an in-depth understanding of your sector and discipline, but, when you scratch the surface, it’s clear that they don’t really understand your area at all. How can someone represent you credibly to potential employers if they don’t really get what you do? Or what you can do? ​ For candidates considering an international move, it’s vital to have people on the ground who know about your target territory. If you are looking to relocate from London to Singapore, you’re about to move somewhere that’s very different in all sorts of ways to what you’re used to. You need someone who’s physically based where you’re going, so they can advise you on all those vital details beyond work, such as tax, housing, and culture. This kind of local knowledge and experience will help you as a candidate to decide if the location is a good fit for you.

Features

  • Recruitment

    Recruitment consultancies are used by many individuals to assist them with finding a new job. Deciding upon a change of career is a daunting experience for many. The question of where to even begin your search can be a cause of stress and confusion.

  • Employmen

    Whether you are unemployed and searching for a job or currently in a role but interested in finding out about potential new opportunities, Power Hunt examines the key issues to consider prior to starting your job search, and the benefits involved in using a third party to negotiate on your behalf.

  • Consultancy

    Choosing the right consultancy is key when considering your next career move. It is essential that the recruiter completely understands your background and has assessed your skills to an extent where they can assist you with your career progression by speaking to you about specific market opportunities.

A good recruiter

A good recruiter will not only speak to you about job opportunities but will advise on interview techniques, CV layout, core competencies for specific roles, salary expectations and progression paths. ​

Consultancy

Does the consultant understand your needs and the business/role you want to work in? It is vital that you feel like you are speaking to credible consultants with relevant industry/market knowledge. Make sure you trust the consultant.
